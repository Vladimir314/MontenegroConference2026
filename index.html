Welcome to the Advances in Modern Topology 2026

We are delighted to welcome you to the Advances in Modern Topology 2026, held for the first time in beautiful Podgorica. The conference will take place in the Main Building of the Montenegrin Academy of Sciences and Arts, a prestigious venue that reflects the spirit of academic excellence.

Aims

The conference aims to foster cooperation, understanding, and collaboration among researchers in Algebraic Topology, Algebraic Geometry, and their applications. Please complete the call for abstracts by the deadline of Friday, August 01, 2026. More information will be posted later.
