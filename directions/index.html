Directions

The conference will be held at the building of the Montenegrin Academy of Sciences and Arts, located in the city center of Podgorica. As the area is central, nearby hotels tend to be slightly more expensive. The closest options include Ziya Hotel, Boscovich Hotel, and the 3‑star Hotel Kerber. A good alternative is Airbnb, which offers affordable stays within walking distance. You can find more information in the logistics document.

For more information, feel free to contact the organizer.
