Directions

The conference will be held at the building of the Montenegrin Academy of Sciences and Arts, located in the city center of Podgorica. As the area is central, nearby hotels tend to be slightly more expensive. The closest options include Ziya Hotel, Boscovich Hotel, and the 3‑star Hotel Kerber. A good alternative is Airbnb, which offers affordable stays within walking distance.

There are two airports in Montenegro:

  • Podgorica airport: taxi is available in front of the Airport (association Aerodrom Taxi Podgorica, which charge 15 Euros to Podgorica). You may order by phone other taxi services that charge less (see below). It takes about 15 minutes to hotel RAMADA, cc. 20 min. to Hotel Hilton, cc. 21 min. to hotel Boscovich, cc. 23 min. to hotel Ziya, and about 23 min. to reach directly MASA.
  • Tivat airport: From Tivat, you will need to take a bus, or taxi transfer to Podgorica. The ride takes up to 2 hours.
  • Dubrovnik airport: It is also an option; the journey to Podgorica takes about 3h30min by bus or transfer.


For more information, feel free to contact the organizer.
